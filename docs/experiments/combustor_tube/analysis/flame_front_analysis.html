Average Flame angle¶

The PDFs shown in Fig. 4 and Fig. 5 were used to find the average flame angle. As a first step, slices of the PDF were taken at different locations in axial direction, see Fig. 6 and Fig. 7. The PDF data was smoothened using a Savitzky–Golay filter with a window length of 101 and a polynomial order to of 3. It was chosen that the peaks of the filtered PDF data seen in the figures correspond to the average location of the flame front in the radial direction.

Fig. 7 PDF of flame front contours of flame 2 at different locations in axial direction.¶
